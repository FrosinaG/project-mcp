Our System

Our System Marco Print distributes an innovative tension frame system, developed in Austria– Europe. Safety first! Through our patented solution, we offer a three-stage safety technology to make the product suitable for the roads. The system consists of the basic profiles – these are bonded to the trailer body using a tested and certified process and connected to the tensioning profiles, which fix the advertising banner securely and safely. Our system allows favorable advertising space using double-sided printing that makes it flexible and cheap. Due to the flexible system, the advertising banner exchange can be easily carried out during a loading process. We will be happy to convince you of our product. FAQ's

NEED HELP?

We’ve shared some of our most frequently asked questions about our services to help you out! If you don’t see your question here , you can always reach us on our contact form

How long is the warranty?

Our frame system warranty is 2 years. With our annual service package, the warranty is extended by one year. The warranty for our high-quality banner is 1 year.

What happens in the event of an accident?

--If the banner is damaged, it can be easily pulled out of the frame. If the system is damaged, the banner must be pulled out. Please contact our service team for assistance.

What are the dimensions of the banners?

The banner size always depends on the trailer. Our team measures each vehicle individually and thus determines the max. banner size.

On which trailers does the system fit?

-Our frame system is suitable for all types of trucks and vans with fixed bodies, WABs, reefer bodies, flat/smooth surfaces (building facades, stadiums, etc.) and containers.

How to install the frame?

-The initial installation of our frame system is carried out by MACRO PRINT certified trained technicians and takes up to 3 hours per vehicle. Procedure:

  1. 1.Cleaning of the surfaces.
  2. 2.Gluing of the base profiles to the surfaces.
  3. 3.Pulling in of the advertising banners into the base profiles.
  4. 4.Fixing of the banners by means of the patented tension profile system.

What happens if I no longer want to use the frame?

-The uninstallation of the tension profiles and banners is possible in the simplest way. Alternatively, new advertising, but also neutral banners can be pulled in, or existing advertising banners can be turned (white background).
